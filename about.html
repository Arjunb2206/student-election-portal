About Amity University Bangalore

Our Vision

To provide education of global standards on the foundation of Indian values and to prepare classless society with equal opportunities for all.

Our Mission

To prepare students with unmatched dedication, offering them comprehensive education that develops their overall personality, making them capable of being positioned globally.

About the Campus

Amity University Bangalore is located on a sprawling campus spread over several acres of land with state-of-the-art facilities. The campus offers world-class infrastructure including modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, libraries, sports facilities, and student accommodations.

Academic Excellence

The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines including Engineering, Management, Law, Communication, Fashion, and many more. Our faculty consists of distinguished academicians and industry experts who are committed to providing quality education.
