BECOME A CANDIDATE

Stand for election and represent your fellow students. Candidate nominations are open until June 10, 2025.

  • Eligibility: Minimum 75% attendance, CGPA of 7.0 or above
  • Positions Available: President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, and Department Representatives
  • Required Documents: Nomination form, Academic transcript, No-Dues certificate
Apply for Candidacy

ELECTION TIMELINE

  • Apr 25 - Jun 10: Candidate nominations
  • Jun 11: Candidate verification
  • Jun 12-14: Campaign period
  • Jun 15-17: Online voting
  • Jun 18: Results announcement

VOTER INFORMATION

  • All registered students are eligible to vote
  • Voting will be conducted through secure online portal
  • Each voter can cast one vote per position
  • Voting credentials will be sent to your university email
  • Results will be published on this portal
Voter Guide

ELECTION COMMITTEE

The election is overseen by an independent committee comprising faculty members and student representatives.

Committee Chair: Dr. Ramesh Kumar (Dean of Student Affairs)

Contact: elections@amity.edu | +91 80 12345678

Report any election misconduct or violations immediately to the committee.

CAMPAIGN RULES

  • No campaigning in academic buildings during class hours
  • Digital campaigns must follow university social media policies
  • Maximum campaign budget: ₹5000 per candidate
  • No negative campaigning or personal attacks
  • All campaign materials must be approved by election committee
