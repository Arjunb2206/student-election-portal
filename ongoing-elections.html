Microsoft Student Ambassador Election Information

The Microsoft Student Ambassadors program is designed to recognize and nurture student leaders who are passionate about technology. Ambassadors represent Microsoft on campus and organize events, workshops, and learning initiatives.

Requirements for Candidates:

  • Must have completed at least one year at Amity University
  • Should have experience with Microsoft technologies
  • Demonstrated leadership skills through past activities
  • Strong communication and organizational skills

Responsibilities of Elected Ambassadors:

  • Organize at least 2 Microsoft-related events per semester
  • Conduct technical workshops on campus
  • Represent Amity at Microsoft regional events
  • Mentor students interested in Microsoft technologies

Election Timeline:

Voting Ends: June 12, 2025 at 11:59 PM

Results Announcement: June 14, 2025

AWS Student Council Election Information

The AWS Student Council is the official representative body of students that works with university administration to address student concerns, organize events, and improve campus life.

Requirements for Candidates:

  • Good academic standing with minimum CGPA of 8.0
  • No disciplinary actions on record
  • Active participation in at least one campus organization
  • Completed at least one full semester at Amity

Responsibilities of Elected Members:

  • Attend weekly council meetings with administration
  • Advocate for student interests and concerns
  • Manage budget allocation for student activities
  • Organize major campus events and festivals
  • Implement initiatives to improve campus life

Election Timeline:

Voting Ends: June 15, 2025 at 5:00 PM

Results Announcement: June 17, 2025

IEEE Student Branch Election Information

The IEEE Student Branch connects students with the global IEEE community, providing access to technical resources, career development opportunities, and a platform to develop technical and professional skills.

Requirements for Candidates:

  • Active IEEE membership
  • Participation in previous IEEE events
  • Good academic standing in technical courses
  • Demonstrated leadership or organizational skills

Responsibilities of Elected Members:

  • Organize technical workshops and competitions
  • Facilitate IEEE membership drives
  • Coordinate with faculty advisor and IEEE section
  • Manage branch finances and resources
  • Represent Amity at IEEE regional conferences

Election Timeline:

Voting Ends: June 16, 2025 at 11:59 PM

Results Announcement: June 18, 2025
