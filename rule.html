Election Rules & Regulations

1. Eligibility:
- Only currently enrolled students with valid credentials may vote
- One vote per student per position

2. Voting Period:
- Voting opens at [9:00/date:01-01-2025] and closes at [18:00/date:01-01-2025]
- No votes accepted after the deadline

3. Voter Conduct:
- No campaigning within voting areas
- No sharing of login credentials
- No coercion or vote-buying allowed

4. Ballot Rules:
- Selections are final once submitted
- No write-in candidates allowed
- Abstention option available for each position

5. Technical Rules:
- Votes are anonymous and cannot be traced to individuals
- System will prevent multiple voting attempts
- Technical issues must be reported immediately

6. Results:
- Winners determined by simple majority
- Results will be certified before announcement
- Recounts may be requested within 24 hours of results

7. Violations:
- Any violation may result in vote disqualification
- Serious offenses may lead to disciplinary action

Note: All decisions by the Election Committee are final.
